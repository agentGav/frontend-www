For our 18th October lecture... Open Source Finance: first steps with peer-to-peer lending

The second of two lectures examining opening up financial data.

What can open data bring to the financial industry? ODI statistician, Ulrich Atz, will show us progress and work to date examining the data released by the UK peer-to-peer lending market under the Show Me the Money project.

He will take us through what it took to get this data released, what it revealed and what lessons could be learnt for the financial sector as a whole.

18th October 2013

1:00pm - 1:45pm
